### Recommendations
1. **Add `api_keys.last_four` index** (P1, ~15 min) — `CREATE INDEX idx_api_keys_last_four ON api_keys(last_four)`. The TODO already has the exact migration.
2. **Remove hardcoded localhost fallbacks** (P1, ~20 min) — Change `config.get('BETTER_AUTH_URL', 'http://localhost:4000')` and `config.get('APP_URL', 'http://localhost:3000')` to `config.getOrThrow` so missing env vars fail fast at boot instead of producing broken URLs at runtime.
3. **Extract session config constants** (P2, ~10 min) — Move magic numbers in `auth.instance.ts` session block to a `SESSION_CONFIG` constant object.
4. **Delete dead exception classes** (P3, ~5 min) — Remove `ApiKeyExpiredException` and `ApiKeyRevokedException` (or wire them into the validation flow if they were intended to be used).
5. **Document or config-drive hardcoded invariants** (P2, ~20 min) — `KEY_PREFIX`, `SUPPORTED_LOCALES`, `SOFT_DELETED_ALLOWED_ROUTES`, and avatar style should either be moved to `config/` or have ADR comments explaining why they are invariant.
6. **Resolve deprecated endpoint** (P2, ~10 min) — Confirm `PATCH /api/roles/members/:id/role` is no longer called by the frontend, then remove it and update docs.
