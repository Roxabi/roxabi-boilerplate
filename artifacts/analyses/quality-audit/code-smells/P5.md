### Summary
P5 (Cross-cutting infra, DB, queue, v1 API) shows several DRY violations from duplicated utility patterns across schema files, email providers, and exception filters, plus one long function in the throttler guard. No critical (P0) issues found. The `common/index.ts` barrel file is dead code.

### Recommendations (prioritized, with effort estimate)
1. **Extract shared schema utilities** (`genId`, `genNanoId`) — `packages/config` or `database/schema/utils.ts`. Effort: 15 min. Reduces 7 copy-paste definitions to 1.
2. **Extract email redaction helper** — `packages/email` or `common/utils/maskEmail.ts`. Reuse the existing `maskEmail` from `emailDlq.handler.ts` in both providers. Effort: 10 min.
3. **Extract shared type guards** (`hasErrorCode`, `extractMessage`) into `common/typeGuards.ts`. Effort: 15 min. Reduces duplication across 4 files.
4. **Refactor `CustomThrottlerGuard.handleRequest`** into per-tier private methods (`handleGlobalTier`, `handleAuthTier`, `handleApiTier`). Effort: 30 min. Cuts the 92-line method to ~30 lines + 3 helpers.
5. **Compose V1 API-key decorator** — `@V1ApiKey()` that bundles `@ApiSecurity('api-key')`, `@RequireApiKey()`, `@UseFilters(V1ExceptionFilter)`. Effort: 15 min. Reduces 15 decorator lines to 3 per controller.
6. **Extract URL path utility** — `getPathWithoutQuery(request)` and `isAuthSensitivePath(path)`. Effort: 15 min. Eliminates 5 duplicated splits + 3 duplicated path checks.
7. **Consolidate error response building** — Make `sendErrorResponse` delegate to `buildErrorResponse`. Effort: 10 min.
8. **Remove dead barrel file** — `common/index.ts`. Effort: 2 min. (Verify nothing else imports it first.)
9. **Extract `errorCodeMapper`** — Shared `statusToCode`, `errorCodeToStatus`, `statusToMessage` mapping. Effort: 20 min. Reduces duplication across v1Exception and domain-specific filters.
10. **Loop-ify `applyRateLimitPreset`** — Iterate over `['RATE_LIMIT_GLOBAL_TTL', ...]` array. Effort: 5 min.
11. **Add branded `DateISOString` type alias** — `@repo/types` or `packages/types`. Effort: 5 min. Self-documents the serialization contract.
